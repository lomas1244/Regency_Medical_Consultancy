ABOUT US

We have been operating from the world famous Harley Street in London, seeing and treating patients both from the UK and around the globe including Film Stars, Royalty and Foreign Dignitaries since 2002. Dr Ruhin Karim is the Medical Director and Lead Physician at Regency Medical. We offer a high-end VIP service to our discerning clients practicing traditional and personalised bespoke general practice. You can choose between seeing the doctor at 10 Harley Street, or the doctor will visit you at your home, hotel, or office.

We know that it is difficult to get an appointment with your NHS GP, and more so, you may not see the same doctor on every visit. By recognising this deficiency, Regency Medical started to offer private services to all our patients since 2002. Furthermore, you may be a visitor or tourist and unexpectedly fallen ill, lost your medications, looking for the right specialist, or in need of an experienced doctor. Flexibility and choice as to how and when you use us, whether this means visiting us as a one-off or becoming a regular patient;

  • You will be always seen by an experienced doctor and never a nurse
  • Appointments are available at times that suits you
  • On-site medical testing and same-day imaging appointments
  • You will have access to a large network of experienced private specialists
  • Same-day Home Visiting Service
Home visiting

Our lead physician Dr Karim offers a high-end VIP home visiting service. He will visit you within the comfort of your home, hotel room, or office. A female chaperone will be available at your request at no extra charge. The service which Dr Karim provides is of the highest standard possible, catering each visit completely to your individual requirements. This service will allocate up to 60 minutes per patient, ensuring a relaxed and non-rushed experience giving adequate time to discuss your case thoroughly, write prescriptions, and make any Specialist referrals.
We carry a mobile pharmacy and most standard and emergency medications can be issued as required. We do not carry controlled drugs.
We also carry rapid diagnostic tests that are especially made for Regency Medical which can be tested during the visit and results given instantly including:

  • Malaria
  • Inflenza A+B
  • Zika Virus
  • Rubella IgG/IgM
  • Prostate Specific Angtigen (PSA)
  • CarcinoEmbryonic Antigen (CEA)
  • Alpha-Feto Protein (AFP)
We are the first home visiting provider offering this unique service.
A full list of other rapid tests can be found in the Sexual Health Screen Service page.

Sexual health screening

Regency Medical offers a completely discreet and personalised sexual health screening service. We offer comprehensive STD screening and sexual health advice.
Screening for sexually transmitted infections (STIs) is an important part of maintaining your sexual well-being, and it is a good idea to get tested if you have unprotected sex, whether you display symptoms or not.  
We offer a wide range of rapid screens for Sexually Transmitted Infections (STIs). Rapid tests carried out at your home include:

  • HIV 1
  • HIV 2
  • Chlamydia
  • Gonorrhoea
  • Syphilis
  • Hepatitis B
  • Hepatitis C
  • Herpes I (NEW)
  • Herpes II (NEW)
  • Bacterial Vaginosis (NEW)
We are the first clinic to be providing a home visiting service with Rapid and instant results during the visit and the first clinic to be able to provide the instant results for Herpes.

Medical Repatriation

Regency Medical offers a repatriation and medical escorting service.
Whether you have been unfortunate to fall ill whilst abroad or whether you need peace of mind with a suitably well qualified doctor escorting you from one place to another, we are here for you to make this journey safe and sound.
We can escort or repatriate you globally from any country to another, using commercial flights. We will provide all necessary equipment required for each individual case.
Just give us a call for your requirements and we will be happy to discuss options with you.

Global case management

Regency Medical offers a discrete and personal VIP Concierge Service. We are here to help you navigate through your medical treatment providing access to the world’s finest Physicians and Surgeons through our database of experts.
We have already done the hard work and sourced the specialist that you may require. After reviewing your notes our team of doctors will use the most appropriate Consultant that you require.
It can be a daunting task for anyone visiting the UK to find the correct Speciality, and seeing various specialists multiple times, with unnecessary costs at every step, some of which are completely avoidable. The doctor reviewing your notes will be able to accompany you to hospital or clinic to see the Specialist. This allows us to be part of your overall treatment and offer a greater understanding of your illness at each appointment. A more informed decision about your treatment can be made reducing the need of multiple opinions and considerable cost savings and time.
Just give us a call for your requirements and we will be happy to discuss options with you.
